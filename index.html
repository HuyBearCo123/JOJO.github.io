JOJO

Created by Hirohiko Araki in 1987, JoJo's Bizarre Adventure is a long-running manga and anime series known for its unique art style, creative battles, and multi-generational storytelling. The series follows the Joestar family across different eras, each member facing supernatural enemies using either martial arts, mystical powers like Hamon, or psychic manifestations called Stands. JoJo constantly reinvents itself, with each new part introducing fresh settings, characters, and themes, from gothic horror to mafia crime sagas. Its innovative blend of action, fashion, music references, and bizarre humor has made it a cultural icon both in Japan and internationally.
